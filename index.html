What is BPA? |

Business Professionals of America is the premier CTSO (Career and Technical Student Organization) for students pursuing careers in business management, information technology, finance, office administration and other related career fields.

What do you do in BPA?

In BPA, we have 3 main competitions: Regionals, State, and Nationals. There are a lot of events to compete in, including events in Business Adminstration, Finance/Accounting, Technology, Video Production, Human Resources, Computer Programming, Graphic and Web Design, and more! You can compete in up to 3 events for regionals, and up to 2 events for state and nationals.
